Description

This hearty and delicious classic lasagna is the ultimate comfort food. Made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, mozzarella, and perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, it’s a family favorite that never disappoints.

Whether you're cooking for a special occasion or just craving something warm and satisfying, this lasagna recipe delivers a perfect balance of flavor, texture, and satisfaction. Serve it with garlic bread and a fresh salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg and a handful of parsley.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat layers, ending with cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
